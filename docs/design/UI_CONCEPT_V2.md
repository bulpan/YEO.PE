# YEO.PE Design Concept V2: Ethereal Radar & Volatile Minimalism

**Theme**: "Echo in the Void" - A map-less experience visualizing signals in the dark.

## 1. Radar Home (The Void)
*   **Concept**: No map. Just you (center pulse) and the signals around you.
*   **Visuals**: Deep black background (#050505). Nearby users are glowing orbs (#00FF94, #7000FF). Distance is represented by size and blur intensity.

## 2. Chat Interface (Volatile Space)
*   **Concept**: Ephemeral conversations that fade away.
*   **Visuals**: Countdown timer (TTL) in monospace. Neon green bubbles for sent messages, glassmorphism for received.

![Chat Interface](/Users/home/.gemini/antigravity/brain/64d957b5-da3f-4ede-b9d7-5bbd4ea7df9e/yeope_chat_interface_1764514428728.png)

## 3. Connection Alert (Signal Matched)
*   **Concept**: The moment of connection.
*   **Visuals**: Sleek glassmorphism modal. High-impact neon glow indicating a match.
