# YEO.PE Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 8, 2026**

YEO.PE ("Service") values your privacy and complies with applicable data protection laws.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Required Information
| Data | Purpose | Retention |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Email address | Account identification, login | Deleted upon account deletion |
| Nickname | Display name in service | Deleted upon account deletion |
| Social login ID | Apple/Google/Kakao/Naver authentication | Deleted upon account deletion |

### Optional Information
| Data | Purpose | Retention |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Profile image | Display in chats | Deleted upon account deletion |
| Push notification token | Sending notifications | Deleted when token expires or account deleted |
| Device information | Push notification optimization | Deleted upon account deletion |

### Automatically Collected Information
| Data | Purpose | Retention |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Chat messages/images | Providing chat functionality | **Auto-deleted 24 hours after creation** |
| Chat room data | Real-time communication | **Auto-deleted 24 hours after creation** |
| Report information | Preventing service abuse | 1 year after processing |
| Block information | User protection | Deleted when unblocked or account deleted |

---

## 2. How We Collect Information
- Direct input during registration
- Social login integration (Apple, Google, Kakao, Naver)
- Automatic generation during app use (messages, push tokens, etc.)

---

## 3. How We Use Information
1. **Service Provision**: Connecting nearby users, real-time chat
2. **Account Management**: Authentication, login, notifications
3. **Service Improvement**: Error diagnosis, anonymized analytics
4. **Safe Environment**: Report/block features, abuse prevention

---

## 4. Sharing with Third Parties
We do not share personal information with third parties without your consent.
Exceptions:
- Legal requirements from law enforcement
- With your prior consent

---

## 5. Service Providers

| Provider | Service |
|----------|---------|
| Oracle Cloud Infrastructure | Server hosting, data storage |
| Firebase (Google) | Push notifications |
| Object Storage | Image storage |

---

## 6. Your Rights
You may request at any time:
1. **Access**: View your stored personal information
2. **Correction**: Modify incorrect information
3. **Deletion**: All data deleted immediately upon account deletion
4. **Processing Restriction**: Request to stop processing your data

**Contact**: support@yeop3.com or [Settings > Delete Account] in app

---

## 7. Security Measures
1. **Encryption**: Passwords and sensitive data encrypted
2. **Access Control**: Limited personnel access
3. **Regular Audits**: Periodic security vulnerability checks
4. **24-Hour Deletion**: Automatic message/room deletion minimizes data exposure

---

## 8. Children Under 14
This Service does not accept registration from children under 14.
If we discover personal information from children under 14 has been collected, we will delete it immediately.
